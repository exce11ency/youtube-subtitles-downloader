How do I download subtitles? +

Simply paste the YouTube video URL into the Subtitle Downloader tool on our homepage. We will fetch all available subtitle tracks for that video, and you can download them in TXT or SRT format.

Are all YouTube videos supported? +

Our tool supports videos that have public subtitles, either uploaded by the creator or auto-generated by YouTube. If subtitles are disabled or private for a video, we won't be able to retrieve them.

What's the difference between TXT and SRT formats? +

TXT format provides a plain text transcript of the subtitles, while SRT (SubRip Subtitle) format includes timestamps, allowing the subtitles to sync perfectly with the video when used in a video player or editing software.

Is there a limit to how many subtitles I can download? +

For free users, there might be certain usage limits or rate limits. Our paid plans typically offer unlimited downloads and access to all tools without restrictions.
