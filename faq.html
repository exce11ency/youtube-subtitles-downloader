Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to your questions about YT Growth and our tools.

Q: Is the YouTube Subtitle Downloader completely free to use?

A: Yes, our YouTube Subtitle Downloader is currently completely free. We aim to keep basic subtitle download functionality free forever, with potential premium features for advanced users in the future.

Q: What subtitle formats can I download?

A: You can download subtitles in two popular formats: SRT (SubRip Subtitle) and TXT (Plain Text). SRT files include timestamps, while TXT files provide the raw text transcript.

Q: Can I download subtitles from any YouTube video?

A: Our tool can download subtitles from any public YouTube video that has either automatically generated captions or user-contributed subtitles enabled. Private videos or videos where the creator has disabled captions cannot be downloaded.

Q: What are the upcoming AI tools?

A: We are actively developing powerful AI-driven tools including a YouTube Video Idea Generator, an SEO Title & Description Optimizer, and a YouTube Keyword Research Tool. These will help you elevate your content strategy significantly.
